BACKGROUND & METHODOLOGY

TRADE BACKGROUND

In recent years, leveraging the “De Minimis” exemption — where parcels valued under €150 are exempt from customs duties — Chinese e-commerce platforms like Shein and Temu have seen explosive growth in the European market.

This “Direct Injection” model bypasses traditional bulk import processes. Billions of small parcels fly directly from China’s core industrial hubs (Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Yiwu) to major European cargo hubs (Liege, London, Frankfurt), reshaping global air freight capacity.
